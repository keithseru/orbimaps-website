Services

GIS and Remote Sensing

Orbimaps has a team of GIS Specialists that provide Commercial & Open-source GIS packages(ArcGIS & QGIS), Spatial Analysis, Mapping, Satellite Image Processing, Mobile GIS, Desktop Solution and Web Mapping.

Surveying and Mapping

Orbimaps has a team of highly qualified surveyors who are fully equiped with RTKs & Total stations. Types of surveys include:Cadastral, Engineering, Geo-Technical, Hydrographic and Utilities.

Construction and Engineering

Orbimaps has a team of skilled engineers and project managers who provide building construction, civil works, structural design, project supervision, and renovation services, ensuring quality, safety, and efficiency across all projects.

Architecture

Architectural services we provide include; Plan designing, Structural drawings, Bills of Quantity, Municipal plan approval, Mechanical and Electrial drawings.

Valuation and Urban Planning

Orbimaps has an experienced team of valuers with expertise in the following: Land/Property valuation, Investment Appraisal, Plant and Machinery valuation, Resettlement Action Plans and Compensation projects.

Property Management

Orbimaps uses property management software developed by Spagetticode Ltd that enables both agents and landlords monitor the state of their properties to enable transparency and efficiency.
